# EU Superstore Executive Sales Dashboard
Tableau Public: https://public.tableau.com/views/EU_Superstore_Executive_Dashboard/Dashboard1?:language=en-GB&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link

## Overview
This project presents an executive KPI dashboard analysing EU Superstore sales performance across regions, categories, and time. The dashboard enables leadership to identify growth drivers, geographic trends, and product performance.

## Business Objective
Providing a high-level view of sales and profit performance across Europe with the ability to drill into category and country performance.

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Median Basket Size
- Total Customers
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Country
- Top Sub-categories

## Insights
- Sales increased 21% YoY, led by Technology
- UK and France contribute the majority of EU revenue
- Phones and Chairs are top-performing sub-categories
- Median basket size declined despite revenue growth
